            History
              Given the diverse expertise of its faculty, the Department of 
              History offers more than seventy-five regularly cycled courses 
              plus numerous special topics and independent study offerings. 
              Undergraduate majors in history select a primary and secondary 
              specialization from the following: American, European, Asian, or 
              Ancient and Medieval history. The Polish Studies Center offers a 
              rich resource for the study of Polish history. In cooperation with 
              other departments, the History Department supports programs in 
              African, African-American, American, Asian, European, Latin 
              American, Middle Eastern, and Women's studies.
              PROGRAMS OF STUDY: BA, BS and MA
