            Modern Languages
              The Modern Language Department offers the BA degree in French, 
              Spanish, Italian, and German, the MA in Spanish and French, and 
              the MS in Spanish. Courses in literature, civilization, 
              composition, and oral expression are integral to all these 
              programs. A major in International Studies with a focus on Russian 
              Studies is also offered. Courses in other languages (Chinese, 
              Japanese, Polish) are offered regularly. Modern Language faculty 
              are active in national and international professional 
              organizations. Each summer they offer a variety of foreign 
              travel/study experiences for credit.
